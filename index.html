About

I want games to look pretty and framerates and workflows to remain smooth.

Hi! I am Ricarda, a freshly graduated media designer with a focus on games based in Germany.

I started creating digital 2D character art at age 15. After a few years of doing commission work I shifted my focus towards the gaming space. My studies at HS Hannover allowed me to expand my skills in 3D, game development, sound design and animation. These new skills have helped me gain a deeper understanding of game creation pipelines, which in turn has allowed me to create better concept/promo art, textures, models and tools.

Through my Bachelor’s project I have also become increasingly interested in tech art, as creating and implementing pleasing game art while keeping assets optimized and creation pipelines efficient was a necessary and rewarding process.

Freelance Work (2019, 2020)

Before finding a fitting university, I was determined to try for a professional carreer in concept art and illustration on my own. At the same time I was taking online courses to keep improving my skills.

At first I was active on Fiverr, but when someone shared a Sea of Thieves fan commission I made for them online, more and more people started contacting me directly.

I was ready to sell prints, keychains and stickers at several conventions as a next step. Unfortunately at that time the pandemic was starting to take its toll and the conventions were canceled – so if you are reading this and are in need of a dusty free sticker, let me know!
