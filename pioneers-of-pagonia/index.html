Concept Art Internship

I was able to secure an awesome concept art internship @Envision Entertainment, where I contributed to the visual development of Volker Wertich's latest fantasy build-up game, Pioneers of Pagonia. Wertich is renowned for creating the original "The Settlers" games.

During my internship, I was responsible for character design and various illustration tasks. This experience taught me a great deal about working within a production pipeline and effectively incorporating feedback. I also gained valuable insights into designing for games and adhering to a directed art style.

Art Direction by Timm Schwank.

This was a compulsory internship for my degree which took place over 19 weeks from 09/2022 - 01/2023.
